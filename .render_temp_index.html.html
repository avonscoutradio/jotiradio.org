Welcome to JOTI Radio

We're the official radio station for World Scouting's biggest event: JOTA-JOTI. You can find out more about the event via the official JOTA-JOTI website.

Over the JOTA-JOTI weekend, we provide live shows, music, competitions, news, entertainment and much more!

We're powered by the lovely people over at Avon Scout Radio, a Scouts Active Support Unit from the United Kingdom.
